Teamwork Directions

Part 1: FEATURE LIST

  • You have 2 minutes. Write your name under “team member” at the top of the collaborative workspace.

  • Introduce yourself to your teammate by writing anywhere in the collaborative workspace.

  • Read each other’s slogans written during the individual task.

  • Write down all your ideas for features of the transportation device as a list. Record them under “Part 1” heading of the collaborative workspace.


Part 2: FEATURE SELECTION

  • You have 1.5 minutes.

  • As a team, select the best features you want to use for the advertisement. Combine your ideas and build off each other. Discuss the merits of your individual ideas come up with ways to merge them.

  • Record the features under “Part 2” heading of the collaborative workspace.


Part 3: WRITING ADVERTISEMENT

  • You have 5 minutes

  • The television advertisement should have a description of the visuals of the advertisement and the action that is happening. Feel free to add additional features, for example “voiceover”, “text on screen”, “speaking”, or “music”. See the sample advertisement under “Task” for an example.

  • Write your advertisement TOGETHER. Each teammate must contribute at least a third of the work - in term of words, ideas, suggestions - we want to see you combining ideas in real time.

  • Do the work under the “Part 3” heading of the collaborative workspace.


Part 4: FINALZING ADVERTISEMENT

  • You have 1.5 minutes.

  • Finalize your advertisement by editing what you've written.

  • Paste the final version under the “Part 4” heading of the collaborative workspace.
